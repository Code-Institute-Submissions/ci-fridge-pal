Understand the difference between best-before dates and use-by dates help reduce the food waste. Best-before dates show when food is at its best quality, but it's usually still safe to eat after. Use-by dates are about safety—food should be consumed by this date to avoid health risks. So, while you can often keep food past the best-before date, use-by dates should be strictly followed.
